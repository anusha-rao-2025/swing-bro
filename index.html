Drill of the Week

The 3:1 Tempo Drill

The ideal swing tempo for most pros is a 3:1 ratio (e.g., 0.75s backswing, 0.25s downswing). This drill builds that perfect, smooth rhythm.

Set your SwinGo app to "Tempo Mode" with a 3:1 ratio goal.

Take slow, half-swings, focusing only on the haptic buzz.

You'll feel a buzz if you're too fast *or* too slow. Adjust until you feel nothing.
