Our Vision: Expanding the Game

SwingBro doesn't just compete within the golf tech market; we expand it.

While competitors sell complex data analytics to seasoned players, SwingBro targets beginners, helping them enter the world of golf with confidence. We sell instant, intuitive learning feedback: no complicated stats, no complex set-up, just feel.

Our long-term vision is to significantly enhance the accessibility and data-driven nature of golf for both novice and experienced players. Currently, golf improvement often relies on costly coaching and limited access to experienced instructors. Our product, SwingBro, eliminates this barrier by integrating coaching intelligence directly into the glove.

SwingBro continuously learns from the player’s swing patterns over time. As the player engages in more practice, the device becomes more personalised, providing tailored feedback and improvement cues rather than generic instruction. For newcomers, this enables affordable, real-time guidance without the need for a trainer; for established players, it offers a silent coach that refines tempo and consistency between lessons and on the course.
